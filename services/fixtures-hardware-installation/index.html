FAQ

Questions about fixtures & hardware

These answers support clarity for visitors and reinforce the page topic for search engines in a natural way.

What kinds of fixture swaps are listed here?

The page focuses on commonly searched handyman-friendly swaps like lights, fans, faucets, bath accessories, cabinet pulls, door hardware, and similar interior updates.

Can you install customer-supplied hardware or fixtures?

For many typical projects, yes. Sending product links, photos, or model information helps determine fit before the job.
